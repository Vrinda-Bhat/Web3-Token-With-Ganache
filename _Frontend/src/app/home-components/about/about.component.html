About the program

The frequent-flyer program of our Air Lines offers points (or "miles") to passengers traveling on most fare types , which accumulate towards free awards such as airline tickets, business and first-class upgrades, and luxury products. Our program uses Blockchain as a service which helps to the customer accumulates generic loyalty rewards within a single "wallet."

Why Blockchain?

Blockchain creates an immutable and time-stamped distributed database entry of every single transaction ever made, making each transaction and its record easily traceable, but also rendering them irreversible, preventing double spending, fraud, abuse, other type of manipulation of the transactions.It has been ranked fairly highly according to some independent news outlets.
